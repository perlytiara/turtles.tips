### What it does
This turtle program automates building the pixel art entrance to a "Donkey Sanctuary" inspired by Genericb's design, as shown in the video. It also includes a time lapse for building additional donkey structures using a special turtle (T17) with enhanced capabilities.

### Setup
- **Requirements**: A fueled turtle (e.g., with coal or other fuel), and sufficient building blocks in its inventory (specific materials depend on the pixel art; refer to the video for details). This program is designed for ComputerCraft in mods like FTB Direwolf20.
- **Installation**: In-game, use `pastebin get Xc98RnqU bDonkeySanctuary` to download the program.

### Usage
Place the turtle at the starting position as demonstrated in the video, ensure it has the necessary blocks, and run `bDonkeySanctuary`. Watch the video for positioning and execution steps, including the second time lapse for extra donkeys.
