**What it does**  
This CC:Tweaked program enables a turtle to automate cactus farming in Minecraft. For details on its functionality, watch the embedded video.

**Setup**  
- Requires a turtle with fuel (e.g., coal) and access to sand blocks and cacti for planting.  
- Download the program in-game using: `wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/BigGamingGamers/cactus-farm.lua` (adjust URL if needed based on the author's repository).

**Usage**  
Place the turtle in the farming area, run `cactus-farm.lua`, and follow any on-screen prompts or video instructions for configuration.

**Tips**  
Ensure the turtle has inventory space for harvested cacti. Refer to the video for optimal layout and troubleshooting.
