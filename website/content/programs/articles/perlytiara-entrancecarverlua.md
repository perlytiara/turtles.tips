**What it does**  
The `entrance_carver` program enables a turtle to carve out entrances or tunnels in your Minecraft world. For full details, watch the embedded video.

**Setup**  
- Use a mining turtle equipped with a diamond pickaxe and sufficient fuel (e.g., coal).  
- Download the program in-game via wget from GitHub: `wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/perlytiara/entrance_carver.lua entrance_carver` (adjust URL if needed based on the author's repo).

**Usage**  
Run the program with `entrance_carver` followed by any required arguments (e.g., dimensions or direction). Refer to the video for specific options and examples.

**Tips**  
Ensure the turtle has inventory space for collected blocks, and position it facing the desired carving direction.
